About the author

Ryan Keeney is a aspiring data scientist with a passion for sports analytics, especially (American) football. After working 6 years as a mechanical engineer at John Deere and designing the Progator GPS PrecisionSprayer, he and his wife, Lydia, took a 1-year leave of absence to volunteer in Paraguay. While they were in Paraguay, Ryan’s position was terminated due to the COVID-19 crisis so they decided to stay in South America. Currently, Ryan and Lydia are enjoying Paraguay and working remotely while Ryan applies to graduate school and learns things like ggplot, SQL, Python, statistics, modeling, and Git.
